Welcome to Dream Car Garage!

Imagine this scenario:

Your great uncle you’ve never even met or heard of died and left you $1,000,000! There is only one caveat…you must spend this money on cars, five cars to be exact. Otherwise you lose your millionaire status as quick as it came. Your great uncle was an avid car collector and enthusiast and wants you to experience the joy of your own car collection. Add each of your five cars to your own “Garage” in the table on the Create page. You choose the color, year, make, model and then enter the cost of each dream machine. Keep the strict million dollar budget in mind, that’s all you get. Then you can create your garage inventory list and view it alongside other enthusiasts collections on the View page.

Share your virtual garage with the world!
